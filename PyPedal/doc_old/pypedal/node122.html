11.9 pyp_nrm

pyp_nrmfunc]pyp_nrm contains several procedures for computing numerator relationship matrices and for performing operations on those matrices. It also contains routines for computing CoI on large pedigrees using the recursive method of VanRaden [VanRadenVanRaden1992].
